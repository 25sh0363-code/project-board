1. Overview
Cancer major health burden. France has comprehensive cancer care through universal healthcare and strong research infrastructure.

2. Context
France has implemented cancer plans since early 2000s focusing on prevention, early detection, treatment quality, and research.

3. Common Types
Breast, prostate, lung, colorectal cancers most common.

4. Risk Factors
Tobacco, alcohol, diet, obesity, environmental exposures, genetics

5. Diagnosis/Treatment
Comprehensive cancer centers, advanced diagnostics, multidisciplinary care, clinical trial access

6. Trends
Steady incidence, improving survival, strong screening programs, advanced treatment access

7. Challenges
Lifestyle-related cancers, treatment costs, health inequities

8. Prevention
Tobacco control, alcohol reduction, healthy lifestyle, vaccination (HPV), screening (breast, cervical, colorectal)

9. Public Health
Successive Cancer Plans, INCa (National Cancer Institute), universal healthcare, research funding

10. Summary
France addresses cancer through comprehensive national plans, universal healthcare, strong research, and coordinated prevention-detection-treatment systems.
