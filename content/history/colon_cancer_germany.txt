1. Overview
Cancer major health challenge. Germany has advanced cancer care infrastructure and strong research base.

2. Context
Comprehensive cancer care through universal healthcare. Strong cancer registry systems and research tradition.

3. Common Types
Breast, prostate, lung, colorectal cancers most common.

4. Risk Factors
Tobacco, alcohol, obesity, diet, physical inactivity, environmental factors, genetics

5. Diagnosis/Treatment
Comprehensive cancer centers, advanced diagnostics including molecular profiling, multidisciplinary care, clinical trials

6. Trends
Steady incidence, improving survival rates, strong screening programs, advanced treatment technologies

7. Challenges
Aging population, lifestyle-related cancers, treatment costs

8. Prevention
Tobacco control, healthy lifestyle programs, vaccination (HPV), screening (breast, cervical, colorectal, skin)

9. Public Health
National Cancer Plan, German Cancer Research Center, comprehensive cancer centers, disease management programs

10. Summary
Germany addresses cancer through universal healthcare, comprehensive cancer centers, strong research infrastructure, and coordinated prevention-screening-treatment systems.
