1. Conceptual Overview
Diabetes mellitus causes chronic hyperglycemia and organ complications. Japan faces increasing diabetes burden alongside population aging.

2. Historical Evolution
Diabetes prevalence has risen since post-war dietary Westernization and lifestyle changes. Japan's universal healthcare provides comprehensive diabetes care and screening.

3. Pathophysiology
Type 2 diabetes (predominant), Type 1 diabetes
Japanese populations may develop diabetes at lower BMI than Western populations

4. Clinical Manifestations
Early detection through health screening systems
Classic symptoms when uncontrolled
Complications including micro and macrovascular disease

5. Risk Factors
Genetics, aging population, dietary changes, physical inactivity, visceral obesity
